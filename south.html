Down south Tour


  • Day 01

    3 nights and 4 days.Watch the sunset & explore the underwater world at Hikkaduwa Beach. The Galle fort is a landmark attraction.National Museum, Historical Mansion Museum and Maritime Museum are located inside the Fort.

  • Day 02


    Whale and dolphin watching in Mirissa is one of the most exciting water activities you can do during your holiday in Sri Lanka. Whale watching season in Mirissa is from November to April.

  • Day 03

    Hummanaya Blow Hole can be accessed by a short walk through a small village near to Matara dotted with local vendors selling handicrafts, street food & refreshing beverages. Udawalawe National Park provides Jeep Safaris for tourists and there are nearly 300 elephants, black bears, water buffaloes and many more animals.
